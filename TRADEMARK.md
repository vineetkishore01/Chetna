## Prohibited Uses ❌

### 1. Competing Services

You may NOT use "Chetna" to offer:
- Hosted/managed Chetna services
- "Chetna as a Service"
- Cloud hosting with "Chetna" in the service name
- Managed infrastructure for Chetna

**Prohibited Names**:
- ❌ "Chetna Cloud"
- ❌ "Managed Chetna"
- ❌ "Chetna Hosting"
- ❌ "Chetna Enterprise Service"
- ❌ "Serverless Chetna"

**Rationale**: These suggest official affiliation and compete with services
the trademark owner may offer.

### 2. Misleading Product Names

You may NOT name products in ways that suggest official status:

**Prohibited Names**:

- ❌ "Chetna Enterprise"
- ❌ "Chetna Plus"

- ❌ "Chetna 2.0"
- ❌ "Chetna Advanced"

- ❌ "Endorsed by Chetna"
- ❌ "The Real Chetna"

## Naming Derivatives

### If You Fork or Modify Chetna

Your project name should make clear it's a derivative, not the official version.

### Acceptable Naming Patterns ✅

```
[YourName] Chetna
Chetna for [Use Case]
Chetna Fork: [Description]
[Project Name] (based on Chetna)
Chetna-based [Description]
```

**Examples**:
- ✅ "Acme Chetna Distribution"
- ✅ "Chetna for Healthcare"
- ✅ "Chetna Fork: High-Performance Edition"
- ✅ "MemoryMax (based on Chetna)"
- ✅ "Chetna-based AI Assistant"

### Required Attribution for Derivatives

Include this notice in your README and documentation:

```markdown
## Attribution

This project is a modified version of Chetna 
(https://github.com/vineetkishore01/Chetna).

Chetna is a trademark of Vineet Kishore and is used for referential 
purposes only. This fork is not affiliated with or endorsed by the 
original Chetna project.

Modifications made: [Describe your changes]
```

### Unacceptable Naming Patterns ❌

```
Chetna [Modifier]
[Modifier] Chetna (if it suggests official status)
Chetna [Version/Number]
```

**Examples**:
- ❌ "Chetna Pro"
- ❌ "Chetna Enterprise"
- ❌ "Chetna 2.0"
- ❌ "Advanced Chetna"

## Enforcement

### Reporting Violations

If you believe someone is violating this trademark policy:

1. **Document the violation** (screenshots, URLs, etc.)
2. **Contact the trademark owner**:
   - Email: vineetkishore01@gmail.com
   - GitHub: https://github.com/vineetkishore01
   - Project Issues: https://github.com/vineetkishore01/Chetna/issues
3. **Include**:
   - Description of the violation
   - Evidence (URLs, screenshots)
   - Your contact information

### Enforcement Process

1. **Initial Contact**: Trademark owner will contact the alleged violator
2. **Good Faith Period**: Reasonable time to come into compliance (typically 30 days)
3. **Escalation**: If no response, may pursue legal remedies
4. **No Waiver**: Failure to enforce doesn't waive future enforcement rights

### Good Faith Compliance

The trademark owner will not pursue:
- Accidental violations corrected promptly
- Good faith efforts to comply
- Non-commercial community content
- Fair use (reviews, commentary, criticism)

## FAQ

### Q: Can I use Chetna in my commercial product?

**A**: Yes! The MIT License allows commercial use. Just comply with attribution
requirements and trademark restrictions.

### Q: Can I offer support services for Chetna?

**A**: Yes, you can offer consulting or support services. Just don't use
"Chetna" in a way that suggests official affiliation (e.g., "Official Chetna Support").

### Q: Can I create a hosted version for my company's internal use?

**A**: Yes! Internal use is fine. The restriction is on offering hosted services
to the public under the "Chetna" name.

### Q: Can I translate Chetna documentation?

**A**: Yes! Community translations are welcome. Just include attribution and
note that it's a community translation, not official.

### Q: Can I create a Chetna plugin or extension?

**A**: Yes! You can name it descriptively (e.g., "Chetna Plugin for VS Code").
Just don't suggest it's official.

### Q: What if I want to use "Chetna" in a way not covered here?

**A**: Contact the trademark owner for permission. Most reasonable requests
will be approved.

### Q: Is "Chetna" a registered trademark?

**A**: It's a common law trademark. Registration may be pursued in various
jurisdictions as the project grows.

### Q: Can I use the Hindi script "चेतना"?

**A**: Yes, the same trademark policy applies to both English and Hindi versions
of the name.

### Q: What happens if I violate this policy?

**A**: You'll be contacted and given an opportunity to come into compliance.
Legal action is a last resort for persistent violations.
